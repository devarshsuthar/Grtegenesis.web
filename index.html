Welcome to GRT GENESIS

GRT Genesis is a Canadian-owned, trusted Distributor and Fabricator of a wide variety of Hi-Performance Mechanical- and Electrical-grade Sheets, Tubes, Rods, Standoff Insulators, Pultruded Shapes and Machined parts in numerous markets and critical applications.

GRT’s machining capabilities are substantial, stock levels of popular Electrical-grade materials are substantial – most importantly our experience and collective knowledge in the wide range of industries we serve is substantial.

GRT’s expertise in Motor Slot Wedges, Ripple-Spring Spacers, Coil Blocks + Shims, Arc Chutes, Panels, Barriers, Slides, Wear Guides, Bus Supports, Rotor and Armature Insulation, Pole Collars and many other critical components has earned us good standing with the largest, most prominent Engineering, Manufacturing, Construction, Maintenance and Repair groups in Motor, Switchgear + Controls, Transformer, Machinery Building, Steelmaking, Renewable Energy, Transit and PowerGen markets globally.

Our ongoing commitment to long-term Customer and Vendor relationships coupled with Continuous Improvement has allowed us to grow and adapt to market demands and changes since 1986.

B.P. Timpano President & CEO
